Consequently, it is believed eating fish will bring us more rich in the coming year.
I guess red envelope is not uncommon to everyone. It's a big allowance from our seniors on the last day of a year.
We put it under our pillow to bless us a happy and safe new year.
We wrote an upside down blessing character on spring couplets due to the similar sound of "upside down" to "coming" in Chinese.
Just like western New Year's Eve, we also stay up late on this day.
However, the reason is to ensure the legendary monster doesn't come, which also brings us the tradition of tomorrow.
Wait for it, we will see it tomorrow.
