Volunteer in Mongolia

2014 Summer

Stories in Mongolia are the most beautiful ones that I've ever experienced. Photos keep moments, and also those wonderful, touching, and amazing stories.
這篇，是想記錄著我2014年到蒙古當志工的一些精彩回憶。 照片可能不是那麼專業，但背後的那些歡笑、驚艷、或是不想想起但忘也忘不了的故事， 才是我最想保留的資產。

         Last summer, I attended volunteer work in Mongolia, where I met endless verdant grass, cerulean blue sky, and lots of true hearts. I stayed two weeks there for teaching kids English, doing construction works ,and exchanging cultural fun facts with other volunteers from various countries. At first, I didn't expect I coudl help those Mongolian kids or I could get some feedbacks from this trip; in stead, I solely wanted to expand my traveling map, to which makes me feel cool if I can add Mongolia. However, it turns out I'm truly falling in love with this charming country and all the stories happened here. It's hard to describe all the touching moments in words, but I still try my best to grasp memories with my camera. This photo gallery shows my life in Mongolia with some texts to share my stories to you.

         經歷近七個小時的飛行，大老遠來到一個聽說會曬很黑、不能洗澡、很乾很遼闊但路很差的國家。 Mongolia，蒙古

         說真的我一開始完全抱著旅遊心態，壓根對farming和teaching沒太大興趣，也從沒想過做志工會是怎麼樣的粗事、怎麼樣的辛苦，就這麼去了，只為了拼自己世界版圖的一角。沒想過會改變世界，更沒想過自己會被改變。獲得了什麼具體說不上來，只知道在回家後和媽媽聊到小孩子們的Good night song竟不可抑止的哽咽了。再看一次在當地寫的日記，與孩子們最深最深的感動還在，很幸運最美的那些幕有很用力的記著。照片無法代表一切，但回憶與故事們我會努力留著。

         底下的照片串流我會慢慢增加此行的照片，與其背後的故事，歡迎點開一一體會我與這國度美麗的邂逅。
